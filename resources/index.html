Crossing Connections

In Path of the Demon Lord, it is reccomended that characters have some sort of tie to the movers and shakers in Crossings. This is a little bit handwavey in the text, and in the interest of speeding up chargen, I added some selectable/rollable options to append to the character creation process to speed it along. I call them connections because I have to call them something.

To use them, at the end of character creation, the player may choose one of their profession categories (Adacemic, Common etc.) and choose a connection of that type. They may either pick one of the listed connections, or roll for it.
